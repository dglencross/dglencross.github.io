David & Rebecca's Wedding

27th April 2019

The King's Arms

27th April 2019

Please arrive by 1:30pm. Ceremony starts promptly at 2:00pm

18 Castle Street, Christchurch, Dorset BH23 1DT

The wedding will take place in the King's Arms Hotel.
The ceremony will be in the pavilion on the green, and the reception will take place in the hotel itself.


Transport and Parking

Trains from London: Waterloo -> Christchurch station, which is a 15 minute walk from The King's Arms.

Parking: If you are staying at The King's Arms, there are a small number of parking spaces, which are available on a first-come-first-served basis. For those not staying at the hotel, please click here for information on local parking.

Accommodation

Rooms are available in The King's Arms for about £165-180.

If you'd prefer to stay elsewhere, there are a few options in Christchurch, including:

  • Travelodge Christchurch (5 minute walk)
  • Premier Inn Christchurch West (15 minute walk)
  • Grosvenor Lodge Guest House (12 minute walk)
  • The Fisherman's Haunt (a short drive away)

    • There are also B&Bs if none of those options suit.

    Alternatively, you can stay in nearby Bournemouth which has lots of options.
